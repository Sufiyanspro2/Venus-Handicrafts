About Venus Handicrafts

Certified Excellence Since 1972
Venus Handicrafts stands as a hallmark of quality and artistic brilliance. We specialize in manufacturing and exporting exquisite decorative handicrafts — Iron, Glass, Brass, Wood, Aluminium, Home Décor, Tableware, and more. Our global presence and ever-increasing acceptance highlight our market goodwill. We blend tradition with contemporary design, delivering exceptional pieces to importers, wholesalers, and retailers worldwide.

Our Craftsmanship Legacy
For over five decades, Venus Handicrafts has been a trusted name in the world of artisanal decor. Our journey began in the cultural heart of Pakistan, where generations of skilled artisans have passed down their techniques through family lineages. Today, we combine these traditional methods with modern design sensibilities to create pieces that resonate with both heritage and contemporary aesthetics. Each creation reflects the dedication and expertise of our master craftsmen who pour their heart into every detail.

Materials & Techniques
We work with an extensive range of materials including premium-grade iron, hand-blown glass, antique-finish brass, sustainably sourced wood, and lightweight aluminium. Each material is carefully selected and undergoes rigorous quality checks before being transformed by our artisans. Our craftsmen employ techniques such as hand carving, metal embossing, glass etching, intricate filigree work, and traditional hammering methods to bring each design to life with authenticity and precision.

Global Reach & Commitment
With distribution networks spanning across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia, we take pride in delivering authentic Pakistani craftsmanship to discerning clients worldwide. Our commitment extends beyond products — we support artisan communities, promote sustainable practices, ensure ethical manufacturing standards, and actively work to preserve traditional craft techniques for future generations. Each piece tells a story of skilled artisanship, sustainable practices, and commitment to timeless beauty. Our mission: enrich spaces with handcrafted soul.
